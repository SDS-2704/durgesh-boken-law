About Adv. Durgesh Boken

Adv. Durgesh Boken has completed more than 26 years of legal practice and has handled over 1,300 matrimonial and family law matters across Gurgaon, Haryana and Delhi.

His practice includes divorce proceedings, child custody disputes, maintenance claims, domestic violence matters, judicial separation, restitution of conjugal rights and other family law proceedings.
